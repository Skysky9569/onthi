Analyze

Analyze
/ˈænəlaɪz/
Verb
Nghĩa: Phân tích, nghiên cứu chi tiết
Từ liên quan: Analysis (noun), Analytical (adj)
"We need to analyze the data carefully before making a decision."
"The scientist analyzed the results of the experiment."

Evaluate

Evaluate
/ɪˈvæljueɪt/
Verb
Nghĩa: Đánh giá, thẩm định
Từ liên quan: Evaluation (noun), Evaluative (adj)
"The teacher will evaluate your performance based on several criteria."
"We need to evaluate the effectiveness of this new method."

🔗 Common Collocations

Make vs Do

MAKE:
• Make a decision
• Make a mistake
• Make progress
• Make an effort
• Make a difference

DO:
• Do homework
• Do exercise
• Do business
• Do research
• Do your best

Have vs Take

HAVE:
• Have breakfast
• Have a shower
• Have fun
• Have a good time
• Have a break

TAKE:
• Take a break
• Take a photo
• Take an exam
• Take notes
• Take place

Get vs Go

GET:
• Get married
• Get divorced
• Get lost
• Get angry
• Get home

GO:
• Go shopping
• Go home
• Go to work
• Go on vacation
• Go out

🔤 Word Formation

Common Suffixes

Suffix Meaning Examples
-tion / -sion Action/state action, decision, discussion
-ment Action/process development, improvement, movement
-ness Quality/state happiness, kindness, darkness
-ity / -ty Quality/state ability, reality, certainty
-ful Full of beautiful, helpful, careful
-less Without hopeless, careless, useless
-able / -ible Capable of readable, possible, flexible
-al Related to professional, emotional, traditional
